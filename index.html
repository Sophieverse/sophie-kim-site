About

photo coming soon

I'm Sophie -- 18, on a gap year before Stanford, and I've been building things for as long as I can remember. I founded my first organization at 12 and two more before I graduated high school as valedictorian of a class of 671.

In 2026, I went from no AI safety experience to youngest person ever admitted to the ERA Fellowship in Cambridge in under two months -- I turned 18 on the flight there.

I come from a long line of people who left everything behind for a chance at something better. My grandfather boarded a boat to America with a few dollars in his pocket, leaving his toddler, infant, and wife behind in rural Korea, because he understood that staying meant stagnation. That urgency is in my blood.

I am currently technically upskilling and preparing to build my first company. There has never been a doubt in my mind that startups are the right path. This isn't ambition -- it's obligation.
